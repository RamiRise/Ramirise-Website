What We Offer

Our Services

From employment solutions and career mentorship to mobile app development and IT projects — RamiRise brings together the expertise your career or business needs to move forward.

Employment Agency

We connect qualified candidates with employers across Toronto and the GTA. Whether you're hiring or job-seeking, our team matches talent to opportunity with care and precision.

Career Placement & Mentorship

Strategic career guidance for professionals navigating the Canadian job market. We offer resume support, interview coaching, and long-term placement in specialized fields.

Internship Programs

Structured internship placements that give students and new graduates hands-on experience in their field, while helping businesses access motivated emerging talent.

Mobile App Development

We design and build custom mobile applications for iOS and Android. From concept and wireframes to launch, our team delivers clean, functional apps tailored to your business goals.

IT Project Solutions

End-to-end IT consulting and project delivery for small and mid-sized businesses. We handle web development, system integrations, software consulting, and technical project management.
